About Me

About Me (Background)) I was born in Irvine, California, and spent much of my life in Japan, where I developed a strong interest in both technology and cross-cultural communication. This diverse background has shaped the way I approach problem-solving, teamwork, and innovation in web development. Currently, I’m pursuing a degree in Software Engineering at BYU-Idaho, with plans to specialize in front-end web development. My goal is to join the web development industry, focusing on website coding and a bit of design, but I’m also interested in expanding into marketing, business management, and e-commerce. While I haven’t worked specifically in these areas yet, I’m eager to learn and integrate them with my technical skills. The combination of web development and business management excites me, and I believe these fields are interconnected and offer plenty of opportunities for growth. Additionally, I value collaboration and prefer working in teams over solo projects. I believe that group work fosters better problem-solving, creativity, and communication, all of which are essential for success in the tech and business worlds.

Contact Me (Call to Action) I’m always open to new opportunities and collaborations. If you believe we could work together or if you’re interested in learning more about my skills and experiences, feel free to get in touch! You can reach me via the contact form or at 123wadayu@gmail.com. I look forward to hearing from you!
